# EnerFeas
**Analyzing feasibility of ecosystems under energetic constraints**

## Summary
Ecosystems can be characterized as complex networks of energy supply, capture, exchange and dissipation. **EnerFeas** is the Julia package that enables quantitative analysis on the feasibility of such networks. By specifying energy fluxes in the system, the package analyze the system's possible energy states by computing the *feasibility domain*. For example, by performing random sampling and volume estimation of the feasibility domains.
